Solntse greet do sedmogo pota,
I bushuet, odurev, ovrag.
Kak u dyuzhey skotnitsy rabota,
Delo u vesny kipit v rukakh.

Chakhnet sneg i bolen malokrovyem
V vetochkakh bessilno sinikh zhil.
No dymitsya zhizn v khlevu korovyem,
I zdorovyem pyshut zubya vil.

Eti nochi, eti dni i nochi!
Drob kapeley k seredine dnya,
Krovelnykh sosulek khudosochye,
Rucheykov bessonnykh boltovnya!
